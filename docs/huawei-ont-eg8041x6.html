Placing the Device

The Huawei OptiXstar EG8041X6-10 ONT can be installed in two ways: on a desk or mounted on a wall. Choose the installation method that best suits your environment.

Installing an ONT on the Desk

The appearance shown in this document may be different from the actual appearance of the product. The actual product prevails.

Steps for Desk Installation:

  1. Choose a suitable location

    Select a flat, stable surface near your network equipment and power outlet. Ensure adequate ventilation around the device.

  2. Position the device

    Place the ONT horizontally on the desk with the indicator lights facing upward for easy visibility.

  3. Ensure proper spacing

    Leave at least 10cm of clearance on all sides for proper heat dissipation and cable management.

  4. Verify stability

    Ensure the device sits firmly on the surface without wobbling. The rubber feet on the bottom should provide grip.

Figure 1-1: Installing on the desk diagram

Important Considerations:
  • Keep away from heat sources and direct sunlight
  • Avoid placing near water or in humid environments
  • Do not stack other devices on top of the ONT
  • Ensure proper cable routing to prevent strain on connectors

Mounting an ONT onto the Wall

Steps for Wall Mounting:

  1. Select mounting location

    Choose a wall location that is easily accessible, near power and fiber optic connections, and away from excessive heat or moisture.

  2. Mark mounting holes

    Use the mounting template (if provided) or measure the mounting hole spacing on the back of the device. Mark the positions on the wall using a pencil.

  3. Install wall anchors

    Drill holes at the marked positions and insert appropriate wall anchors for your wall type (drywall, concrete, etc.).

  4. Mount the device

    Insert screws through the mounting holes on the back of the ONT and secure it firmly to the wall anchors.

  5. Verify installation

    Ensure the device is level and securely mounted. Test by gently pulling to confirm it's firmly attached.

Figure 1-2: Wall mounting installation diagram

Pro Tips:
  • Use a level to ensure the device is mounted straight
  • Consider cable routing path before final positioning
  • Leave enough slack in cables to allow for service access
  • Mount at a height that's easy to view indicator lights

Connecting Cables

Follow these steps to properly connect all necessary cables to your Huawei OptiXstar EG8041X6-10 ONT.

1

Power Connection

Connect the provided power adapter to the DC power input port. Ensure the voltage matches the specifications (typically 12V DC).

Port: DC 12V Power Input
2

Fiber Optic Cable

Carefully connect the fiber optic cable from your service provider to the PON/OPTICAL port. Handle with care to avoid bending the fiber.

Port: PON/Optical Port (SC/APC)

Never look directly into the fiber optic port. Laser light can damage eyes.

3

Ethernet Connection

Connect Ethernet cables from the LAN ports to your computer, router, or other network devices using standard Cat5e or Cat6 cables.

Ports: 4× Gigabit Ethernet (RJ45)
4

Telephone Connection (Optional)

If voice services are included in your plan, connect a telephone to the POTS/TEL port using a standard telephone cable.

Ports: 2× POTS/Voice Ports (RJ11)
5

USB Connection (Optional)

The USB port can be used for firmware updates or connecting USB storage devices for file sharing.

Port: USB 2.0 Type-A

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about the Huawei OptiXstar EG8041X6-10 ONT.

What is an ONT and why do I need it?

An ONT (Optical Network Terminal) is a device that converts fiber optic signals from your Internet Service Provider (ISP) into electrical signals that your devices can use. It's essential for fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) internet connections, providing high-speed internet, voice, and potentially TV services.

How do I access the ONT's web management interface?

To access the web management interface:

  1. Connect your computer to one of the LAN ports on the ONT
  2. Open a web browser and enter the default gateway IP address (typically http://192.168.1.1 or http://192.168.100.1, or as specified by your ISP)
  3. Enter the default username and password (usually found on the device label or provided by your ISP)
  4. You should now have access to the configuration interface

Note: Some ISPs may restrict access to the management interface. Contact your ISP if you cannot access it. If your device uses HTTPS, your browser may show a security warning due to a self-signed certificate; you may need to review and accept this warning to proceed.

The PON light is red or off. What should I do?

If the PON indicator is red or off, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check that the fiber optic cable is properly connected to the PON port
  2. Ensure the fiber cable is not bent sharply (minimum bend radius: 30mm)
  3. Clean the fiber connector with a proper fiber optic cleaning tool if available
  4. Power cycle the ONT (unplug power, wait 30 seconds, reconnect)
  5. If the issue persists, contact your ISP as there may be a service outage or provisioning issue

Can I use my own router with this ONT?

Yes, the EG8041X6-10 can work in bridge mode, allowing you to use your own router:

  1. Access the ONT's web interface
  2. Navigate to Network → WAN settings
  3. Configure the WAN connection to bridge mode (this may require ISP credentials)
  4. Connect your router's WAN port to one of the ONT's LAN ports
  5. Configure your router with the ISP-provided PPPoE or DHCP settings

Note: Configuration options may be limited based on your ISP's policies.

How do I change the WiFi password?

To change your WiFi password:

  1. Log in to the ONT's web interface
  2. Navigate to WLAN → Security Settings
  3. Select your wireless network (2.4GHz or 5GHz)
  4. Change the WPA Pre-Shared Key (password)
  5. Click Apply or Save to confirm changes
  6. Reconnect your wireless devices with the new password

Tip: Use a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Can I disable the WiFi function?

Yes, you can disable WiFi if you prefer to use a separate wireless router:

  1. Access the web management interface
  2. Go to WLAN → Basic Settings
  3. Uncheck "Enable WLAN" or toggle the WiFi switch to off
  4. Apply the changes

Disabling WiFi can reduce power consumption slightly and eliminate potential wireless interference.

What should I do if I forgot my admin password?

If you've forgotten the admin password:

  1. Try the default password listed on the device label
  2. Contact your ISP for the current admin credentials
  3. As a last resort, perform a factory reset by pressing and holding the reset button for 10+ seconds (this will erase all custom configurations)

Warning: Factory reset will require reconfiguration by your ISP.

How do I perform a firmware update?

Firmware updates are typically managed automatically by your ISP through remote management (TR-069). Manual updates may not be available or recommended. If you need to update firmware:

  1. Contact your ISP to inquire about firmware updates
  2. They may push an update remotely or provide specific instructions
  3. Never install unofficial firmware as it may brick the device or violate your service agreement
